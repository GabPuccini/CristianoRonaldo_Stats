1 Introduction

Welcome to RonaldoStats.app ("we", "our", or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect and process your data when you visit our website.

2 Information We Collect

We collect information through Google Analytics, which includes:

  • Pages you visit on our site
  • Time and date of your visit
  • Time spent on each page
  • Your approximate location (country or city level)
  • Device type (mobile, tablet, or desktop)
  • Browser type
  • Referring website (how you found us)

We do NOT collect any personal information such as names, email addresses, or IP addresses.

3 How We Use Your Information

We use this data to:

  • Understand which statistics are most popular
  • Improve our website's performance and user experience
  • Fix technical issues
  • Plan new features based on user interest

4 Cookies

We use cookies to collect analytics data. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. You can control cookies through our cookie banner when you first visit the site.

Types of cookies we use:

  • Analytics cookies that help us understand how visitors use our site

5 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Reject cookies using our cookie banner
  • Delete cookies through your browser settings
  • Browse our site with cookies disabled (the site will still work)
  • Request information about data we've collected
  • Request deletion of your data

6 Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data. Google Analytics data is stored on Google's secure servers and we have no access to individual user information.

7 Third Party Services

We use Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google LLC. Google's privacy policy is available at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

8 Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9 Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by an updated "Last updated" date.
